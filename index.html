Vubble AI

Customer Discovery & Market Pivot

2017–2019

Vubble was an AI-powered video curation startup helping audiences discover trustworthy content amid information overload. The company initially targeted education, but revenue constraints demanded a strategic rethink.

I led customer discovery research across two phases, working directly with the co-founder. In the first phase, I conducted 20+ qualitative interviews across education stakeholders. When the data revealed limited buying power, I pivoted to researching media & entertainment buyers—interviewing executives at CBC, Bell Media, and Corus Entertainment.

Key insight: Education buyers didn't see content vetting as urgent (they overestimated students' "digital native" abilities). Media companies felt the pain acutely—one poorly sourced video in a documentary could mean reputational and legal damage. This research directly informed Vubble's pivot to media & entertainment.
